Account

Why do I need to create an account to place an order?

Creating an account allows you to save your credit/debit card and shipping details, view your shopping history, track the status of your order etc. It also helps us recommend products based on your browsing details.

What can I do with my account?

You can provide your feedback and ratings for the product you have received. You can also create a wishlist for yourself where you can add items for your later purchases and view recommendations that we make based on your previous purchases.


My Orders

How many items can I add to my cart?

You can add any number of items to your cart.

What is the estimated delivery time?

You can expect to receive your order within 2-15 days of placing your order.

Note: The delivery time depends on product availability, product type and size, shipping destination, and seller’s location.

How do I track my order?

To track your order:

  1. Log on to your account and go to the Track Order page.
  2. Enter your Tracking Id/Order Id and Email Id and click Show Details. A pop-up window with your order details appears.

You can also track your order by providing your Tracking Id/Order Id and Email Id details in the Track Order section on the Home page.

How do I cancel my order?

To cancel your order :

  1. Log on to your account and go to the My Orders page.
  2. Select the item you want to cancel, indicate the reason for cancellation and click Cancel Order.

Note: After your cancellation request is processed, the payment will be refunded in 5 business days.


Returns & Refunds

What is the return period policy?

You can return or replace an item within 15 days of receiving the ordered item.

Note: Return period after delivery is not applicable if the product is defective or damaged.

When will I get my refund?

Refunds are initiated after we have received the returned product. The amount will reflect in your account in 5 business days.


Payments

What methods of payment can be used?

You can make a payment using either your credit or debit card. You can also opt for the Cash-on-Delivery (COD) option.

Why is the COD option not offered in my location?

Availability of the COD option depends on the ability of our courier partner, delivering at your location, to accept cash as payment at the time of delivery.

Why do I have to pay delivery charges?

Delivery charges are those charges that sellers take to deliver the product to the shipping address.

Note: Delivery charges vary by sellers and shipping destination.
